# Rollio Account Deletion

Updated: 2026-03-03

You can permanently delete your Rollio account in-app:

1. Open `Settings`.
2. Open `Account`.
3. Tap `Delete account`.
4. Confirm the warning prompts.

What deletion does:

- Deletes your Rollio account data from active app datasets, including library, graph, sessions, and account settings.
- Signs you out after deletion.
- Does not guarantee recovery of deleted data after completion.

Notes:

- Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
- Operational backups/logs may retain records for limited periods for reliability/security purposes.
- Local backups stored on your device are not uploaded and can be removed from your device storage.
- Deletion of account data does not automatically create a refund right; refund eligibility is governed by app-store policy and applicable law.
