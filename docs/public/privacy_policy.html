Rollio Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-03

Rollio ("we", "us") provides a mobile app for organizing Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training content.

Data We Collect

  • Account data:
    • Email address (used for account creation, authentication, and account management).
  • User content:
    • Technique names and notes.
    • Position tags and graph relationships.
    • Training/journal entries.
    • Optional video links you add (for example YouTube or Instagram URLs).
  • App settings:
    • Theme preference, belt display preference, sharing preference, and related profile settings.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate users and keep accounts signed in.
  • Store and sync your training data across sessions/devices.
  • Provide app functionality (library, graph, add/edit/delete flows, backups/restores).
  • Maintain service reliability and protect account/security integrity.

YouTube API Services Disclosure

Rollio uses YouTube API Services for video suggestion and metadata features.

By using YouTube-related features in Rollio, you are also subject to:

  • YouTube Terms of Service: https://www.youtube.com/t/terms
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Data is processed/stored with infrastructure providers required to run the service (for example authentication/database/cloud hosting providers).
  • User-enabled sharing features may send selected technique metadata into the app's Review Queue for possible inclusion in the Master Catalog.

Retention and Deletion

  • Account and app data are retained while your account is active.
  • You can delete your account in-app from Settings -> Account -> Delete account.
  • On account deletion, account data is permanently removed from active app datasets, subject to operational backup/log retention windows required for system integrity and incident recovery.
  • Local backups created on your device are stored locally and can be removed by uninstalling app data or deleting local storage content.

Service Continuity and Data-Loss Risk

  • We do not guarantee uninterrupted service, uninterrupted access, or permanent availability of all features.
  • We may modify, suspend, or discontinue all or part of the service at any time.
  • Although we use reasonable safeguards, no storage or transmission system is fully risk-free and data loss can still occur.
  • You are responsible for maintaining your own backups of important content.

Security

  • Data is transmitted over encrypted network connections (HTTPS).
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards for stored data.

Children's Privacy

Rollio is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can review and remove data as required.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updated versions become effective when published with a new effective date.
